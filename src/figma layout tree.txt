- USE OF DATA (Frame Layer)
  - To provide you with the most accurate and personalized virtual try-on experience, our solution creates a realistic 3D avatar based on your body profile. This avatar helps simulate how clothing will look and fit on your unique physique. To achieve this, we use a combination of manual input and advanced machine learning techniques based on the following personal data: 1. Age Range Your age range helps us estimate typical body composition associated with your life stage. This ensures your avatar reflects realistic proportions and characteristics. 2. Height Establishes the overall scale of your avatar and provides proportional accuracy across limbs and torso. 3. Weight Used alongside height to determine overall body mass and shape, which impacts clothing fit and avatar realism. 4. Body Shape Helps us model the overall silhouette and balance of your avatar’s torso, hips, and shoulders. 5. Fitness Level Provides insight into muscle tone, body composition, contributing to a more lifelike and personalized avatar. 6. Main Measurements (Chest, Waist, Hips) Key for defining torso proportions and ensuring a precise digital fit. 7.
